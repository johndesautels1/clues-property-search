CONFIDENCE SCORING GUIDELINES
High Confidence:

3+ authoritative sources agree
Official government record
Recent MLS data (<30 days)
Visual confirmation (photos, permits)

Medium-High Confidence:

2 authoritative sources agree
County permit with specific date
Recent listing data (30-90 days)

Medium Confidence:

Single authoritative source
Market averages/proxies
Calculated values from reliable inputs
Older data (90-365 days)

Low Confidence:

Single non-authoritative source
City-wide average applied to property
Proxy from similar properties
Outdated data (>1 year)
